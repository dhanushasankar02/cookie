sweet preservation

Cookie Care & Storage

Follow our pastry chef's guide to keep your treats tasting bakery-fresh for longer.

Room Temperature

Store in an airtight glass jar or cookie tin for up to 2 weeks. Avoid direct sunlight and moisture to keep icing colors vibrant and cookie textures perfect.

Freezing Cookies

Wrap individually in clingfilm and place in a zip-lock bag. Freeze for up to 3 months. To serve, thaw completely in wrapper to prevent condensation on royal icing designs.

Reheating Guidelines

For cookies with warm gooey centers (like chocolate chunk), bake at 150°C (300°F) for 2 to 3 minutes, or microwave for 8-10 seconds. Enjoy fresh-baked warmth instantly!

quick answers

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before reaching out.

How far in advance should I place a custom order? +
We recommend 7–14 days for custom cakes and large cookie sets. For smaller batches (2–3 dozen cookies), 4–5 days is enough. Rush orders may be available with an extra fee – just ask!
Do you offer shipping outside the city? +
Yes, we ship nationwide via insulated packaging for cookies and select baked goods. For cakes, we currently offer local delivery & pickup. Contact us for shipping estimates.
Can I request a vegan / gluten-free design? +
Absolutely! Our studio specializes in dietary adaptations – vegan buttercream, gluten-free flour blends, and nut-safe preparation. Mention your needs in the form above.
What's your response time on queries? +
We aim to reply to all emails and form submissions within 24 hours, Monday–Saturday. For urgent calls, our support line is +91 98765 43210 (9am–8pm).
