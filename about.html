Precision & Passion

The Sculpting Process

How we transform simple ingredients into edible masterpieces inside our sterile design workshop.

The Foundation: Precision Baking

Dough is rolled to exactly 6mm thickness to ensure a completely flat, non-spreading surface. Baked in customized convection stone ovens for a perfectly even, gold-standard crust that holds sharp, crisp edge profiles.

The Canvas: Flooding

A porcelain-like base is laid down using our signature egg-white royal icing. Flooded with a precise 12-second consistency fluid that levels itself out, then cured under climate-controlled dehydrators for 18 hours to form a high-sheen satin finish.

The Palette: Watercolor Painting

Our decorators use paintbrushes, calligraphy nibs, and sponges to hand-paint directly onto the dried royal icing canvas. Pigments are hand-blended using natural food dyes derived from botanical flowers, beetroot, spirulina, and turmeric.

The Finish: Micro-Piping & Gilding

The final layers of dimensional detail are piped using fine-tip nozzles (as thin as 0.5mm) to construct laces, pearls, and outlines. Lastly, elements are finished with delicate strokes of 24k edible gold leaf or silver flakes for a luxury glow.
