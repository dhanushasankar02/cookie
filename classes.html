Our Bakery Journey

2009 — The First Loaf ✨

A small home kitchen, a wood-fired oven, and an obsession with perfect crust. CrumbCraft was born from pure passion for the craft of baking.

2012 — Our First Storefront 🏡

We opened our doors on Maple Street — a warm space with a display counter, fresh-baked aromas, and a growing community of loyal customers.

2015 — Custom Cake Studio 💍

We launched our bespoke wedding & celebration cake studio — designing edible art for life's most cherished milestones.

2018 — Pastry & Viennoiserie 🥐

We brought in a classically trained pastry chef to expand our French repertoire — laminated doughs, éclairs, and buttery croissants baked fresh each dawn.

2021 — Baking Workshops 🎓

Have a question or want to place a custom order? We’d love to hear from you. Visit our bakery, call us, or reach out online — fresh cakes and warm service await you.

2025 — Bakery House 🚀

A brand-new flagship location — a full production kitchen, cake consultation lounge, in-house café, and online delivery launching citywide.
